Description
This is the largest demo I ever released. I worked on it for several months but actually finished it half an hour before the demo-compo deadline at The Party-91. Features some nice effects such as the Glenz/Twist vector, Z-rotating bobs (not shown), a bunch of different fractals and a lightsourced logo that acutally got a few cheers from the audience :) It was no match for the demos like Hardwired and Oddysey that blew everything else away at that party though.
